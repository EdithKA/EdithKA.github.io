About the project

Lend a hand to those who need it most

Isao's Journey is a game developed together with colleagues from the DDVJ degree at the UJI, with the goal of informing and engaging younger audiences about the important work of Doctors Without Borders.

Accompany our protagonist through diverse and varied territories in his important mission to help those most in need. Exploration, investigation and mini-games — all woven into a heartfelt adventure made by G9Devs.
