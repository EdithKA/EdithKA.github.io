About me

A little about Edith

I'm a game designer and developer with a deep love for storytelling, interactive worlds, and the space where art meets code. I believe games are one of the most powerful mediums for human connection.

My work spans from narrative-driven experiences to 3D character modeling, always with the same question in mind: what does this feel like to inhabit?

I've collaborated on projects for humanitarian organizations, built interactive web experiences, and crafted games that linger.
